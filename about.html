About

Remember that story you started writing but cannot complete because of your writer's block? Or all those journal entries you have on your computer? It could even be an idea that could one day pay off all your student loans; of course you wouldn't want someone reading this and stealing it away. We're talking about all that text information you'd like to keep to yourself. It might all be password protected, but don't you have a nagging feeling that someone might leak them all out like Hillary Clinton's emails?

Encryption is simply a way to ensure that only those authorized can read the information. It usually involves a key, which you need to protect just like a password since everyone with this key would be able to decrypt the data and read it. Our website lets you encrypt and decrypt any .txt file so you don't have to worry about your data getting leaked everywhere. Just protect your key and try not to forget it as you would not be able to decrypt the information.

"Once you've lost your privacy, you realize you've lost an extremely valuable thing."
